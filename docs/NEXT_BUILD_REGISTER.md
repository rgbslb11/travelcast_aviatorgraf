### Phase D — AviaImpact + RoadCast Products

**Type:** New scoring products and AviatorGraf tabs.

**Do not start until Phase C is committed and audited.**

#### D1 — AviaImpact Score v0.1
Aviation flight-impact score: 0/5–5/5

Formula:
```
AviaImpact =
  Terminal Weather Score      × 0.30
+ Convective / Hazard Score   × 0.20
+ FAA / NAS Constraint Score  × 0.20
+ Airport Exposure Score      × 0.10
+ Time-of-Day / Bank Score    × 0.10
+ Route / Airspace Exposure   × 0.10
```

Hard overrides:
- FAA ground stop active → force 5/5
- FAA ground delay program → minimum 4/5
- Airport closure → minimum 4/5, force 5/5 if effectively unusable
- Convective SIGMET directly over terminal → minimum 3/5

Tables: `aviaimpact_airports`, `aviaimpact_source_runs`, `aviaimpact_weather_samples`, `aviaimpact_nas_constraints`, `aviaimpact_scores`

Anti-hallucination rule: AviaImpact must not claim ground stop, exact delay minutes, runway closure, or cancellation risk unless sourced from FAA/ATCSCC/NOTAM/official data.

#### D2 — RoadCast
Highway corridor weather-impact score: 0/5–5/5

Formula:
```
RoadCast =
  Forecasted Weather Score   × 0.40
+ Heavy Truck Route Score    × 0.10
+ Day-of-Week Score          × 0.10
+ Holiday / Vacation Score   × 0.15
+ Average Traffic Score      × 0.15
+ Route Danger Score         × 0.10
```

Hard overrides:
- Official closure/impassable/do-not-travel → force 5/5
- Official chain law / traction law → minimum 4/5
- No live road-status source → do not claim closure

Tables: `roadcast_corridors`, `roadcast_source_runs`, `roadcast_forecast_samples`, `roadcast_scores`

50-corridor seed file: `data/reference/roadcast_corridors.csv`

Source: NWS api.weather.gov forecasts only (official source). Never Baron/OpenWeather/commercial for public claims.
